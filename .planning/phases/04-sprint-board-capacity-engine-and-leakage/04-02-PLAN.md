Implementation steps: fetch sprint with release (for meetingPercentage, supportPercentage), active squad members, absences with member relation, holidays, and app settings. Count business days (Monday-Friday, inclusive) between sprint startDate and endDate. Gross hours = sum for each active member of (memberDailyHours * businessDays) where memberDailyHours is `workingHoursFullTime` for FULL_TIME and `workingHoursIntern` for INTERN. Absence reduction = for each absence whose date range overlaps the sprint, sum (overlapping business days * that member's daily hours). Holiday reduction = (active member count * average daily hours per active member * number of holidays falling on business days within the sprint). Capacity after absences = gross - absenceReduction - holidayReduction. Meeting reduction and support reduction are conceptual (applied as percentages), so net hours = capacityAfterAbsences * (1 - meetingPercentage - supportPercentage). Net days = netHours / standardDayHours. Add `tests/coverage.test.ts` with test cases: (a) 2 full-time members, 10 business days, no absences/holidays/meetings/support => gross 160h, net 160h, net 20d; (b) one member on vacation for 2 business days => gross 160h, absence 16h, net 144h; (c) 1 holiday on a business day with 2 members => holiday reduction 16h; (d) 10% meeting + 20% support on 300h after absences => net 210h; (e) net hours 210 / standardDayHours 8 => 26.25 days.
  </action>
  <acceptance_criteria>

    - 10% meeting + 20% support on 300h after absences yields netCapacityHours = 210.
