**As a** squad planner, **I want to** close and reopen sprints while the system handles unfinished stories and records leakage, **so that** historical sprint truth is preserved and scope continuity is maintained.

## Objective

Implement sprint closure with rule validation, automatic unfinished-story migration to the next sprint (creating one if needed), leakage history recording, and sprint reopening with preserved history.

Purpose: Capture the real outcome of a sprint execution.
Output: Closed sprints retain finished stories; leaked stories carry history; closed sprints can be reopened.

## Success Criteria

1. Closing a sprint validates that status is IN_PROGRESS and shows a confirmation modal with finished/unfinished counts.
2. Finished stories (status DONE) remain in the closed sprint.
3. Unfinished stories move to the next sprint preserving their status (SPRINT_BACKLOG or IN_PROGRESS).
4. If the closed sprint is the last one, a new sprint is created automatically with correct dates and PLANNED status.
5. Each leaked story creates a LeakageHistory record with origin sprint, destination sprint, event date, and status at event.
6. Reopening a closed sprint changes status to IN_PROGRESS and preserves all leakage history.
7. Sprint board shows leakage indicators on story cards and disables editing for closed sprints.
