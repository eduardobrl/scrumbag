## Phase Goal

**As a** squad planner, **I want to** execute a sprint on a drag-and-drop board while adding stories from backlog, **so that** I can track story progress through the sprint lifecycle.

## Artifacts this phase produces

- `src/features/sprints/sprint-board.tsx` — Three-column board UI (Backlog da Sprint, Em Execucao, Finalizado)
- `src/app/api/stories/[id]/status/route.ts` — Story status update endpoint
- `src/features/sprints/add-story-dialog.tsx` — Backlog story selector with capacity preview
- `tests/sprint-board.test.ts` — Unit tests for board status mapping
- `tests/e2e/sprint-board.spec.ts` — E2E tests for drag-and-drop and add-story flows

## Objective

Deliver the operational sprint board with fixed columns, native HTML5 drag-and-drop status changes, and backlog story assignment with preview. This is the first vertical slice of Phase 4, giving users an interactive board before capacity and closure features arrive.

Purpose: Replace the sprint detail placeholder with a working execution surface where stories move through statuses and backlog items can be added.
Output: Working board UI, status persistence API, and add-story dialog integrated into the sprint screen.
